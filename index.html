Wake Up to Better Coffee with Blue Tokai

Tired of stale, boring coffee? Blue Tokai brings freshly roasted beans from India's best farms straight to your doorstep. Every cup is full of flavor, aroma, and character.. like having a café in your kitchen.


Coffee with a Story

We source beans from over 80 Indian farms, each with its own unique soil, altitude, and climate. That means every batch tells a story.. some fruity, some chocolaty, some bold.. and your mornings never taste the same twice.


Roasted Right, Every Time

Our roasters use top-notch machines and check every batch for quality. Whether you love a light, delicate roast or a strong, dark one, you'll get consistent flavor that makes every cup worth it.


Brew Your Way

Whole beans, pre-ground, single-origin, or blends.. you choose. And if you don't have fancy coffee gear, no worries. Our Channi coffee makes brewing simple, even with a basic tea strainer.


Coffee at Your Doorstep

Ordering is easy online, via our app, or through delivery platforms. Or, visit a Blue Tokai café to pick up a fresh batch. Fresh coffee. No compromises.


Make Every Sip Count

With Blue Tokai, it's not just coffee.. it's a better start to your day. Fresh, flavorful, and just how you like it.
